What is HydroSmart?

HydroSmart is a smart water bottle that helps people remember to drink water. It connects to an app that tracks how much water you drink each day.

The Problem

Many people forget to drink enough water during the day. This can lead to tiredness, headaches, and low energy.

The Solution

HydroSmart reminds you to drink water and shows how much you still need.

Features

  • Tracks daily water intake
  • Sends reminders to drink water
  • Simple mobile app connection
  • Helps build healthy habits

Why it matters

Drinking enough water helps your brain, energy, and focus. HydroSmart makes it easy to stay healthy without thinking about it all day.
